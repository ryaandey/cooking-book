BUTTER CHICKEN (MURGH MAKHANI)

Butter Chicken is a creamy, mildly spiced Indian dish with marinated chicken simmered in a rich tomato-based sauce.



Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (boneless)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Spices: garam masala, kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)


    • Instructions:

    • Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Coat the chicken and marinate for 30 minutes to 2 hours.

    • Sear the marinated chicken in butter until lightly browned, then set aside.

    • In the same pan, cook tomato puree, spices, and cream. Add the chicken, simmer for 10 minutes, and finish with butter and kasuri methi.
