SAMOSA

A samosa is a popular Indian snack that is enjoyed in many parts of the world.



Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water, as needed

For the Filling:

  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (optional)

    • For Frying:

    • Oil for deep frying


    Instructions:

    • Mix flour, salt, and oil. Add water to form a smooth dough. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.

    • Heat oil, add cumin seeds, ginger, and green chilies. Stir in mashed potatoes, peas, and spices. Cook for a few minutes and cool.

    • Roll the dough into circles, cut in half, and form cones. Fill with potato mixture and seal.

    • Deep-fry samosas in hot oil until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.

    • Enjoy hot with chutney.
